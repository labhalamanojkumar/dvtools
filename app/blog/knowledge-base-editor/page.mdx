Excellent documentation is a cornerstone of a great product. A well-organized knowledge base empowers your users to find answers on their own, reduces the load on your support team, and helps new customers get up to speed quickly. However, creating and maintaining high-quality documentation can be a challenge without the right tools.

Our **Knowledge Base Editor** is a powerful, yet easy-to-use platform for building and managing your entire help center. It combines a modern, rich-text editor with smart organizational features to make documentation a joy to write and read.

## What is a Knowledge Base Editor?

A Knowledge Base Editor is a specialized content management system (CMS) designed for creating documentation. It provides a What-You-See-Is-What-You-Get (WYSIWYG) editor that allows you to write and format articles with text, images, videos, and code blocks. It also includes features for organizing articles into categories, managing versions, and making the content easily searchable for end-users.

## A Common Use Case: Documenting a New Feature

Your team is about to release a major new feature for your SaaS application, and you need to prepare the documentation for it.

1.  **Create a New Category:** In the Knowledge Base Editor, you create a new category named after the new feature, for example, "Automated Reporting."
2.  **Write the Articles:**
    *   You create a "Getting Started" article that provides a high-level overview of the feature. You embed a short demo video.
    *   You write a detailed "How-To Guide" that walks users through the process of creating their first report. You include numbered steps and annotated screenshots.
    *   You add a "Reference" article that explains all the available settings and options in detail, using tables and code blocks.
3.  **Organize and Review:** You arrange the articles within the "Automated Reporting" category. You then invite a colleague to review the drafts. They leave comments directly in the editor suggesting a few clarifications.
4.  **Publish:** Once the feedback is incorporated, you publish the entire category. The new articles are now live in your help center, ready for users on launch day.
