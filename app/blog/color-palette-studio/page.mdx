## The Power of a Great Color Palette

Color is one of the most powerful tools in a designer's arsenal. It evokes emotion, establishes brand identity, and guides user attention. But creating a harmonious, effective, and accessible color palette from scratch can be a daunting task.

That's where our **Color Palette Studio** comes in. It's a free, all-in-one tool for designers and developers to create, visualize, and test stunning color schemes for any project.

## What is the Color Palette Studio?

## A Workflow for Perfect Palettes

1.  **Find Your Inspiration:** Start by picking a primary brand color or uploading an image that captures the mood you're aiming for. The studio will extract the dominant colors.
2.  **Generate and Refine:** Use the generator to create a complementary scheme (e.g., analogous, triadic, complementary). Lock the colors you love and regenerate the others until you're satisfied.
3.  **Build Out the Shades:** For each of your core colors (primary, secondary, neutral), generate a full range of shades from light to dark. These will become your `blue-100`, `blue-200`, ..., `blue-900`.
4.  **Check for Accessibility:** Use the live preview and contrast checker. Ensure your primary text color has a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 against your main background color. Adjust shades as needed to pass WCAG AA standards.
5.  **Export and Implement:** Once you're happy with the entire system, export it in your desired format and integrate it directly into your project.
